Custom Ear Plug Services

Whether you are in the film or music industry or an avid concert or loud nightclub attendee, we proudly offer Westone etymotic earplugs to protect your hearing.

These custom designed earplugs come in three levels of attenuation (9 dB, 15 dB and 25 dB) that reduce loudness equally at all frequencies without any degradation in fidelity.

Etymotic earplugs are made of a medical grade silicone and will provide you with years of uninterrupted performance. The manufacturer provides one set of filters for the level of attenuation that you select. Additionally, a second plug for maximum sound reduction is supplied at no charge for situations that require sound deadening when loss of fidelity is not an issue.

We have provided thousands of these plugs since their introduction in the 1990s.
